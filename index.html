06 · FAQ

Questions teams
actually ask us.

Don't see yours? We answer every inbound personally — usually within a working day.

Ask us directly
Do you work with non-technical teams?+
Most of our best engagements are with non-technical leaders — ops, finance, legal, revenue. We translate constraints into systems and handle every line of code, eval, and deployment ourselves. Your team owns the workflow; we own the build.
Which models do you build on?+
We're model-agnostic and risk-aware. Claude Opus / Sonnet for reasoning-heavy work, GPT-4-class for breadth, local models (Llama, Mistral) when data residency matters. We pick per use case, not per logo.
How do you handle data security?+
We default to your infrastructure, your keys, your audit logs. We're SOC 2 aligned in our own practices, sign mutual NDAs and DPAs, and have shipped systems under HIPAA-grade redaction. We can also operate fully on-prem or in your VPC.
What happens after the engagement ends?+
You own the codebase, the prompts, the evals, and the runbooks. We document everything, train your team for two weeks, and stay on call for 60 days. After that, ~70% of clients move to the Embed tier — but only because they want to, not because they have to.
How fast can we start?+
A 30-minute intro call this week, a written proposal within 48 hours, kickoff within 2 weeks of signed SOW. We deliberately keep capacity tight — 4 active builds at any time — so we can move fast on the ones we take.
Can you replace a full-time AI hire?+
Often, yes — for the first 12-18 months. Embed gives you senior judgement and shipped systems at roughly half the loaded cost of a senior AI engineer, with no hiring lag. When you're ready for a full-timer, we help you scope the role and interview candidates.
