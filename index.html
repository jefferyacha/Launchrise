How We Work

A consulting-first approach to AI.

We start at your business model. The tools come after. Three stages, every engagement.

01

Discover & Diagnose

Understand first, automate second.

We learn how your business actually works. Goals, constraints, handoffs, what a win looks like. We map your stack and where your data lives. We find the real bottlenecks before we touch a tool.

02

Design, Build & Validate

Custom workflows. Tested before launch.

We pick the handful of problems where AI will actually pay off. We design and build on real data, test multiple approaches, and explain every choice in plain language. Nothing ships until it's proven in a real environment.

03

Launch, Monitor & Optimize

We don't build it and disappear.

We launch with clear success metrics and safeguards. We monitor, collect feedback from your team and customers, and fix issues fast. We refine prompts, logic, and models so the system keeps up as your business changes.

Asked & answered

Questions teams actually ask us.

If yours isn't here, the answer is probably "yes, send the email." We reply to every inbound personally, usually within a working day.

Ask us directly
i.Do you work with non-technical teams?
Yes. Most of our best work is with operations, finance, and revenue leaders, not engineers. You bring the problem, we turn it into working software, and we own every line of code, every safety check, and every deployment ourselves.
ii.Whose data is this, exactly?
All yours. We build inside your accounts, your tools, your logs. Mutual NDAs and DPAs come standard. On premise when you need it.
iii.Which AI are you using?
Whichever fits the job. We match the model to the problem, balancing reasoning, breadth, cost, and your data constraints. When your data can't leave the building, we run local models on your own infrastructure. We pick per problem, not per logo.
iv.What happens after the engagement ends?
You own the codebase, the prompts, the evals, and the runbooks. We document everything, train your team for two weeks, and stay on call for sixty days. About 70% of clients move to a custom engagement afterwards.
v.How fast can we start?
Intro call this week. Written proposal within 48 hours. Kickoff within two weeks of a signed SOW. We keep four active builds at a time, on purpose, so we can move fast on the ones we take.
vi.Can you replace a full-time AI hire?
For the first 12 to 18 months, often yes. Working with us costs roughly half of a senior AI engineer fully loaded, with no hiring lag. When you're ready for a full-time hire, we'll help you scope the role and interview candidates.
vii.Where are your clients based?
We work with teams across Europe, Africa, the Middle East (Saudi Arabia, UAE, and the rest of the GCC), and Asia. Industries lean toward education, logistics, professional services, and building automation. We're not religious about it.
