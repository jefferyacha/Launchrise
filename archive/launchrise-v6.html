How we work

A methodical, low-risk way to ship AI.

Three stages. We start by understanding your business, not by jumping straight into tools.

01

Discover & Diagnose

Understand first, automate second.

We learn how your business actually works. Goals, constraints, handoffs, what a win looks like. We map your stack and where your data lives. We find the real bottlenecks before we touch a tool.

02

Design, Build & Validate

Custom workflows. Tested before launch.

We pick the highest-impact opportunities. We design and build on real data, test multiple approaches, and explain every choice in plain language. Nothing ships until it's proven in a real environment.

03

Launch, Monitor & Optimize

Continuous improvement, not a drop-and-go.

We launch with clear success metrics and safeguards. We monitor, collect feedback from your team and customers, and fix issues fast. We refine prompts, logic, and models so the system gets sharper as your business evolves.

Asked & answered

Questions teams actually ask us.

If yours isn't here, the answer is probably "yes, send the email." We reply to every inbound personally, usually within a working day.

Ask us directly
i.Do you work with non-technical teams?
Yes. Most of our best engagements are with operations, finance, and revenue leaders. Not engineers. We translate the constraint into the system, and we own every line of code, every safety check, and every deployment ourselves.
ii.Whose data is this, exactly?
All yours. We build inside your accounts, your tools, your logs. Mutual NDAs and DPAs come standard. On-prem when you need it.
iii.Which AI are you using?
Whichever fits the job. Claude for the reasoning-heavy stuff, GPT for breadth, local models like Llama when your data can't leave the building. We pick per problem, not per logo.
iv.What happens after the engagement ends?
You own the codebase, the prompts, the evals, and the runbooks. We document, train your team for two weeks, and stay on call for sixty days. About 70% of clients move to a custom engagement afterwards. They want to, not because they have to.
v.How fast can we start?
Intro call this week. Written proposal within 48 hours. Kickoff within two weeks of a signed SOW. We keep four active builds at a time, on purpose, so we can move fast on the ones we take.
vi.Can you replace a full-time AI hire?
For the first 12 to 18 months, often yes. A custom rate card with us costs roughly half of a senior AI engineer fully loaded, with no hiring lag. When you're ready for a full-time hire, we'll help you scope the role and interview candidates.
vii.Where are your clients based?
We currently work with teams across Europe, Africa, the Middle East (Saudi Arabia, UAE, and the rest of the GCC), and Asia. Industries lean toward education, logistics, professional services, and building automation. We're not religious about it.
