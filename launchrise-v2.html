ii. process

Boring on purpose.
Predictable on delivery.

A six-week sprint, repeated until the system runs itself. No discovery decks, no perpetual roadmaps, no AI-transformation theatre.
step one · week 0

Listen

We sit with operators, not slide decks. Three questions: where are humans bottlenecked, what's already broken, and what would you stake budget on?

→ a one-page brief
step two · weeks 1–4

Ship

Working v0 in week one. Loom walkthrough, eval set, cost-per-run benchmark. Then we harden — logging, retries, guardrails, real integrations.

→ a production system
step three · weeks 5–6

Hand back

Your team owns the codebase, the prompts, the runbooks. We document, train, and stay on call for sixty days — long enough to see second-order wins, short enough never to be a dependency.
